Fynbos & Fern — homepage directions

Three directions from the brief ("warm, grounded, quietly expert — protea silhouettes, Cape granite, weathered timber; nothing glossy, nothing lime-green"). Each names a palette, type intent, band rhythm and hero concept, composed only from sections the registry actually has. v0.1 presented 2026-07-21 → refinements below → Direction A approved.

*Straw = ornament only (fails text AA on paper: 2.41). Text #243018 / muted #57614A / on-ink #C9D2BE / soft clay on ink #E4AC7C.

Type & shape

  • House pairing retained: Fraunces 600 display (its italic = the botanical accent voice), Albert Sans text.
  • No pills. Timber-and-granite geometry: btn 0.5rem, field 0.375rem, card 0.75rem.

Band rhythm

  • Paper → Mist alternation; Olive Ink once per page (testimonials), twice max with the final CTA.
  • Edges: shore + tide as the house pairing (soft dune profiles), swell reserved for the entry into ink.

Hero concept

We plant what belongs here.
Split hero: garden photo right, promise + consult CTA left.
Book a consultation

Depth: escape-crop portraits on ink; straw dot-grids in corners only. Motion: fadeUp/staggerReveal, no pinning.

v0.1 → v0.2 refinements (measured): clay deepened #A15A28 → #8A4A1F — v0.1 measured 4.50:1 on Sage Mist (zero AA margin for inline links) and 5.24 for button text; v0.2 clears at 5.86 and 6.82. Straw demoted from "small accents" to ornament-only after measuring 2.41 on paper.
